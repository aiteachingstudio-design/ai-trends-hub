"Write a LinkedIn post about [Achievement/Lesson Learned]. Start with a powerful 1-line hook that creates curiosity. Use short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max) with plenty of white space. End with an engaging question to drive comments. Do not use generic emojis."

"Create a detailed video script outline for a YouTube video titled '[Your Title]'. Give me a 30-second high-retention intro hook, followed by a chronological breakdown of 5 core value points, and a seamless call-to-action transition at the end."

Prompt 4: Newsletter Welcome Sequence

"Write a welcome email for a new subscriber to my newsletter about [Niche]. Keep it warm, personalized, and human. Share a brief 3-sentence origin story, explain exactly what value they will get every week, and ask them one simple question to reply to."
